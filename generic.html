Myself

I’m Waskar Miguel Paulino, an Afro-Latinx software engineer specializing in web development. My journey began as a self-taught coder while working as a Genius at Apple, where I discovered the power of blending liberal arts and technology. Growing up as a first-generation American, I was inspired by my Dominican parents’ dreams, which pushed me to pursue my own. Along the way, I’ve gained technical troubleshooting, project management, and Spanish translation skills, and developed a strong knack for customer success. My background in music and design sets me apart, which gives me a unique, artistic approach to problem-solving. I view challenges as creative, collaborative opportunities, and I’m passionate about bringing this perspective to help others achieve their web development goals, especially those who lack the resources to do so.
